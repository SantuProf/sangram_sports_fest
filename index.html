UEMJSports-fest

All the students are hereby informed that the inter-house sports fest Sangram Sports Fest is going to be organized in the institute from 28th september 2025 to 29th september 2025. Our annual inter-college sports festival is in line with our University OF Engineering & Management, Jaipur sports persona to one’s technical excellence ensuring an all-round development of an individual. In the last few years, the undying efforts of the students and the support from the institute have helped us establish SANGRAM as a brand name. SANGRAM has captured eyes in its boundaries, but this year we aspire to make its presence felt wide over in the hearts of the students. We expect your kind support towards making this year’s festival an even greater success, establishing a benchmark of sports activities. We promise to put in our maximum efforts to achieve the goal.
